BioAPI Reference Implementation: Contacts

The main points of contact for BioAPI are the mailing lists.

For any technical issues regarding the reference implementation, there is the bioapi-ref mailing list. To subscribe, send to majordomo@lists.bioapi.org with a body of "subscribe bioapi-ref". Subscription is open to anyone.

For administrative issues regarding the reference implementation, there is the bioapi-ref-admin mailing list. To subscribe, send to majordomo@lists.bioapi.org with a body of "subscribe bioapi-ref-admin". Again, subscription is open to anyone.

For any issues regarding these mailing lists, contact the list manager at bioapi-ref-owner@lists.bioapi.org.

There is also a mailing list which is open only to members of the BioAPI Consortium. Inquiries regarding joining the Consortium should be directed to the BioAPI Secretary at secretary@lists.bioapi.org.
